Support for Filipino state university students to empower future leaders across the regions. This program aims to provide financial assistance to deserving students who demonstrate academic excellence and commitment to community service.

Requirements

  • Must be a Filipino citizen
  • General Weighted Average (GWA) of 85% or higher
  • Currently enrolled in a state university or college
  • Proof of family income (not exceeding ₱250,000 annually)
